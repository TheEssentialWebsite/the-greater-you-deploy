Seven Areas of Wellness

The Greater You's proprietary Seven Areas of Wellness framework provides a holistic roadmap for personal transformation across every aspect of your life.

Body

Physical health, fitness, nutrition, sleep, and energy management as the foundation for well-being.

Mind

Cognitive clarity, learning, mental flexibility, and growth mindset development.

Spirit

Purpose, values alignment, faith or philosophy, and connection to something greater.

Feel

Emotional intelligence, regulation, self-awareness, and psychological resilience.

Connect

Relationships, communication, community, and meaningful social bonds.

Invest

Financial wellness, career development, time management, and resource stewardship.

Serve

Contribution, generosity, mentorship, and giving back to others.
