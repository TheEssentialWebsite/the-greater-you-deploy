Why Positive Psychology Coaching Works

Positive Psychology Coaching uses science-based frameworks like the PERMA Model, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), and resilience training to support measurable, lasting growth.

  • Authentic Connection — Strengthen relationships through evidence-based communication strategies and emotional intelligence.
  • Cognitive Clarity — Overcome limiting beliefs and develop mental agility through cognitive reframing techniques.
  • Purpose-Driven Living — Define and pursue meaningful goals aligned with your values and strengths.
  • Emotional Resilience — Build the capacity to navigate adversity and maintain well-being during challenging times.

The Science Behind Our Approach

Our framework combines Dr. Martin Seligman's PERMA+ Model with The Greater You's proprietary Seven Areas of Wellness. This integration bridges science and real-world transformation across emotional, mental, physical, and relational health.
